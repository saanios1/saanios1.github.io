Instructions:

In this game you have the chance to design your dream office!

You can choose the background from two options above, or start from a blank slate.

You can use the furniture below - move them around the office and adjust their size and orientation! You can duplicate or delete pieces of furniture using the buttons on the right. You can also use the drawing tool. On the right you can select different coloured pens! You can move your drawing around just like you can move furniture. If you want to select more than one line at a time, you can click and drag over to use a selection box.

I hope there is room for me in your dream office.
